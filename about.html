About Limi P lake

What is Limi Plake?

LimiPlake is a world where learning never has limits. It’s a home for explorers who love both music and coding — a place where creativity and logic meet.

Through LimiPlay, students can learn piano in fun and musical ways. Through LimiMake, they can dive into the world of programming — writing code, solving problems, and building cool things from scratch.

But LimiPlake isn’t just about lessons. It’s about imagination — about discovering that playing a note and writing a program both start with the same thing: an idea.

At LimiPlake, we believe every learner can dream, create, and grow — from their first note to their last line of code.

The Story

Once upon a time, I made a poster in kindergarten that said, “When I grow up, I want to be a piano teacher.” It was just a drawing with crayons and big dreams — but even back then, I already knew I loved teaching, creating, and helping others learn something new.

As I grew older, that dream didn’t fade — it grew with me. In the summer of 2021–2022, while most kids were playing outside, I started a tiny club called Anhad’s Piano and Coding Lessons. It began in my home, with just a few students and a big idea: to bring together the two things I loved most — music and programming.

At first, the lessons were simple. We played beginner piano pieces, learned little bits of code, and talked about how both are like magic — where you press something, and something beautiful happens. But I wanted my club to have a name that sounded official. So I called it PCLWA — Piano and Coding Lessons With Anhad. That name was already taken!

Not giving up, I tried again — this time, PACLA — Piano and Coding Lessons by Anhad. But that one was taken too! Instead of stopping, I decided to dream bigger.

I wanted a name that didn’t just describe what I do, but what I believe in. One day, while sketching ideas, a word popped into my head — LimiPlake. It felt fresh, colorful, and full of possibility. I decided it would mean “limitless learning.”

Now, LimiPlake isn’t just my project — it’s my dream come true. It’s a world where every learner can play their first song, write their first program, and discover the joy of making things. From a crayon poster in kindergarten to a real company with lessons, books, and ideas — that’s how LimiPlake began: from a dream on a poster to a place where everyone can learn from the first note to the last line of code.
